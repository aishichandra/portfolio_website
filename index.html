AI search has a citation problem

Comparing eight AI search engines and their ability to cite news sources.

We tested eight AI search tools—including ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, Gemini, and Copilot—with 200 real news excerpts. In over 60% of cases they failed to return the correct article, title, or URL. Many gave confident wrong answers or broken links. The report has been covered by Nieman Lab, Ars Technica, Axios, and El País.
